Our Story

The BloFish slogan, All 4 All, is the belief that everyone should have the same opportunities in life, no matter your gender, race, sexual preference, religious beliefs, or abilities. The slogan and message is ingrained deeply into the company's culture as well as its customers, as 10% of every purchase goes directly to charity.

BloFish donates to a charity each month, with the charities switching monthly. Check us out on our instagram @blofishclothing for more information, and see the charity of the month.

For us at BLoFISH Clothing, "All for All" isn't just our slogan, it is our lifestyle; one we live and breathe every moment.
